DDR Finder

This is a work in progress proof of concept tool for finding DDR machine locations (or arcades in general). No warranty is made regarding operation, and no accuracy of results is guaranteed. Machine location data collected from Zenius -I- vanisher.com and DDR-Navi (no affiliation with said sites, data snapshots may not be up-to-date). No donations accepted; donate to the above sites instead, without them this app would not have been possible!
